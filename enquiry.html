Frequently Asked Questions

What items can be recycled?

We accept paper, cardboard, plastic, glass, and metal. All items must be clean and dry before submission. Please remove any macro-particles or contaminants.

How often do you collect waste?

Our refuse trucks collect waste twice a week on established routes. Contact us to arrange a collection schedule for your area.

Do you offer commercial recycling services?

Yes, we provide recycling solutions for businesses of all sizes. Please contact us for a customized quote.

Can I volunteer with GreenWave Gurus?

Absolutely! We welcome volunteers for our community initiatives, tree-planting events, and clean-up days. Submit an enquiry and let us know your interests.

How can I stay updated on events?

Follow us on social media or contact us directly. We announce all upcoming community events, workshops, and volunteer opportunities regularly.

What is your mission?

Our commitment is to preserve our earth through community upliftment by creating work opportunities while ensuring sustainable use of natural resources for future generations.
