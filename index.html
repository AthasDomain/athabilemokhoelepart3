Did You Know?

  • recycling saves energy - one glass bottle recycled has the potential to save enough energy to brighten up a light bulb for well over 3 hours
  • recycled plastic containers are turned into: cellphone cases, equipment, clothes and rugs
  • recycled cartons - makes paper towels, toilet paper and roof cover boards
  • recycling teaches you that one man's trash is another man's treasure
