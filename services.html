Services Provided

Greening

Every week we strive to plant 20 trees all around our community. By the end of the year we hope to have achieved the plantation of over 1000 trees.

Waste Collection

There are systems in place for collection which include:

  • Refuse trucks with permanent drivers for collections twice a week
  • Established routes for collection are put in place

Recycling Waste Products

At GreenWave Gurus we prioritise recycling because we simply are converting materials considered trash into new, usable products. Recycling allows us to follow nature's rhythm through our input as a society to conserve natural resources, reduce greenhouse gas emissions and conserve energy.
We know how to do it. Now we as a community have to decide how to execute that long term objective towards a brighter, greener and cleaner future. To start recycing as much as currently possible is the only way forward from an environmetal and financial perspective.

Maintenance of Landfill Sites

  • Each landfill site is staffed with caretakers

Eradication of Alien Invasive Species

Alien invasive species and all trees of that sort need to be eradicated as this pertains to sustainable environmental growth.

Community Upliftment

We have a community upliftment program in place to create work opportunities for the unemployed and to further educate our community on the importance of recycling and how to go about it.

Note: Please make sure that all items given to be recycled are dried out properly and are free from any macro-particles to support efficient recycling.
