Services Provided

  • Greening
      • Every week we strive to plant 20 trees all around our community. By the end of the year we hope to have achieved the plantation of over 1000 trees.
  • Waste Collection

    • there are systems in place for collection which include:
      refuse trucks with permanent drivers for collections twice a week
      established routes for collection are put in place
  • Recycling Waste Products
      • At GreenWave Gurus we prioritise recycling because we simply are converting materials considered trash into new, usable products.
  • maintainance of landfill sites
      • each landfill site is staffed with caretakers
  • Eradication of alien invasive species
      • alien invasive species and all tress of that sort need to be eradicated as this pertains to sustainable environmental growth.
  • Community Upliftment
      • we have a community upliftment program in place to create work opportunities for the unemployed and to further educate our community on the importance of recycling and how to go about it.

    please make sure that all items given to be recycled are dried out properly and are free from any macro-particles to support efficient recycling
