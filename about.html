We follow KEY LEGISLATIONS for environmental protection and sustainability.

  • National Environmental Management Act (NEMA)

    • The foundational law for environmental governance, requiring Environmental Impact Assessments for development projects and pollution control measures.

  • National Waste Management Strategy (NWMS)

    • A comprehensive strategy for managing waste in a manner that minimizes environmental impact and promotes sustainability.

  • National Environmental Management: Waste Act (NEMWA)

    • Governs waste management, recycling and disposal practices.

  • National Environmental Management: Air Quality Act (NEMAQA)

    • Regulates air quality, sets emissions standards and protects against air pollution.

  • National Environmental Management: Biodiversity Act (NEMBA)

    • Focuses on conservation of ecosystems like wetlands and forests, protected areas and species including endangered ones.
