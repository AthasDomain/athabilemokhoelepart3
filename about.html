Key Environmental Legislations:

National Environmental Management Act (NEMA)

The foundational law for environmental governance, requiring Environmental Impact Assessments and pollution control.

National Waste Management Strategy (NWMS)

A strategy for managing waste to minimize environmental impact and promote sustainability.

National Environmental Management: Waste Act (NEMWA)

Governs waste management, recycling and disposal practices.

National Environmental Management: Air Quality Act (NEMAQA)

Regulates air quality, sets emissions standards and protects against pollution.

National Environmental Management: Biodiversity Act (NEMBA)

Focuses on conservation of ecosystems like wetlands and forests and protects endangered species.
